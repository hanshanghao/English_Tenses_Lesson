Exam Tips & Strategies

Proven techniques to boost your GEPT score

⏰

Time Management

Divide your time: 30% reading, 30% writing, 20% listening, 20% review. Never spend more than 2 minutes on one question.

📝

Note-Taking

During the listening test, jot down keywords — who, what, when, where, why. Use abbreviations to save time.

🔍

Read the Questions First

For reading sections, scan questions before reading the passage. This helps you locate answers faster.

🗣️

Speak Out Loud

Practice speaking daily for 10 minutes. Record yourself and listen — it builds fluency and confidence.

📖

Build a Word Journal

Write down 5 new words daily with example sentences. Review them every weekend for retention.

🧘

Stay Calm

Deep breathing before the exam lowers anxiety. Remember: you have prepared well — trust your training.
