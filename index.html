1. Learning Objectives

Students will be able to:

  • ⏰ Understand what tense is — the time of an action
  • 🔍 Identify present, past, and future tenses
  • ✍️ Use simple sentences in each tense
🧠

2. Warm-Up (5 minutes)

Teacher writes on the board — are these the same? 👇

🟢 I eat an apple.
🔵 I ate an apple.
🟡 I will eat an apple.

👉 Tense tells us when something happens!

📚

3. The Three Tenses (15 min)

🟢 Present Tense
Now / Every Day
Action happening now or every day
  • I eat rice.
  • She plays soccer.
  • We go to school.
Signal words: every day now always
🔵 Past Tense
Yesterday / Before
Action already finished
  • I ate rice.
  • She played soccer.
  • We went to school.
Signal words: yesterday last night
🟡 Future Tense
Tomorrow / Later
Action that will happen later
  • I will eat rice.
  • She will play soccer.
  • We will go to school.
Signal words: tomorrow next week
✏️

5. Practice: The Time Game (10 min)

Teacher says: "I play." — students change it!
🟢 I play → 🔵 I played → 🟡 I will play
Try these:
eat→ ate→ will eat
go→ went→ will go
👥

6. Pair Activity (10 min)

Students ask and answer each other:


What do you do every day?

I play games every day.

What did you do yesterday?

I played games yesterday.

What will you do tomorrow?

I will play games tomorrow.
📝

7. Worksheet — Fill in the Blanks

  • 1 I played soccer yesterday.
  • 2 She eats lunch now.
  • 3 We will go to the park tomorrow.
💡 Hint: Use played, eats, and will go — look at the signal words!
🎤

8. Speaking Practice

Listen first, then repeat out loud!

🟢 Today I go to school.
🔵 Yesterday I went to school.
🟡 Tomorrow I will go to school.
🧩
